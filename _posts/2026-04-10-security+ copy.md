## Motivation
CompTIA Security+ is a foundational requirement that nearly every company demands for junior-level cybersecurity positions. It is an essential certification for establishing professional credibility and securing an entry-level role in the industry.

## What does it include? (SY0-701)
1. General Security Concepts
Focuses on the core principles of the CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability). It covers essential security controls, basic cryptography, and authentication methods.

2. Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations
Teaches how to identify various attack types such as social engineering, malware, and network exploits. It also focuses on analyzing system vulnerabilities and implementing proper mitigations.

3. Security Architecture
Covers the design of secure environments across networks, cloud, and mobile platforms. You learn how to place security appliances and apply data protection strategies effectively.

4. Security Operations
Emphasizes hands-on practical skills like incident response procedures, log monitoring, and using security tools for vulnerability scanning and threat hunting.

5. Security Program Management and Oversight
Deals with the administrative side of security, including risk management, developing corporate security policies, and ensuring compliance with international laws and regulations.

## Exam Cost, Date, and Method
The standard exam voucher costs $404 USD. You can obtain discounts through the CompTIA Academic Store if you have a student email or by purchasing vouchers from authorized training partners. To avoid paying renewal fees, you can simply earn a higher-level certification (such as CySA+ or CASP+) which automatically renews your Security+ status. Exams can be scheduled for any available date and taken either at a physical Pearson VUE testing center or from home through online proctoring.

## How I am studying and how I feel about it
I dedicate one hour every day to studying official practice questions. I am now on my fourth round of review. I have noticed that the exam content is highly factual; the questions are essentially the same and only differ in phrasing. Consequently, I believe that if I were provided with just one week of dedicated, full-time study, I could successfully pass the exam by the end of that week.
